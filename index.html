I am currently a postdoctoral researcher at the University of British Columbia (UBC), working with Prof. Tony Yang. I will join 2012 Lab as a Research Scientist, where I will work on foundation models.

I received my PhD from the University of British Columbia (UBC), where I worked with Prof. Zhengbo Zou and Prof. Omar Swei. Prior to that, I received my M.A.Sc. and B.Sc. degrees from Southeast University.

My research interests include Reinforcement Learning and Robotics.
